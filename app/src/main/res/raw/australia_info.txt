Known for being in the "Down Under", Australia is its own continent that contains around 25 million people. Their capital city is Canberra, located on the southeastern coast of the country. Australia was the second to last continent discovered by Europe the first being Antarctica. The Great Barrier Reef is a huge attraction sheltering all kinds of sea life year round. The unique sights of the reef are slowly declining due to global warming. Similar to The United States, Australia's currency is also a dollar and the current exchange rate is: 1 USD to 1.46 AUD. There are numerous ways to travel within Australia, you can travel in plane, car/bus, or train. The Crown Towers in Sydney is the only six star hotel in Australia, a most memorable place to stay at!
