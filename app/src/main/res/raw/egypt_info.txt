Egypt is located on the northeastern part of Africa with a population of just over 100 million people. Their capital city is Cairo which sits on the longest river in the world, the Nile river. This country has a very deep and complex history whether its the complexity of the pyramids or the history of early humanity. Holstering one of the world wonders Egypt cannot be replaced. The Pyramids need no introduction as one of the seven world wonders found in Egypt. Egypt has immense amounts of history leaving some of the oldest ruins known to man. The Egyptian Pound is the national currency of Egypt and it is worth 0.052 USD right now. The Sofitel Winter Palace is a phenomenal place to stay at being just off of the world famous Nile river. Busses are the primary source of transportation while riding camels is always an option as well!
