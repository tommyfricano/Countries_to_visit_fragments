Italy is located in southern Europe with a population of 58.4 million people. Their capital city is Rome located on the central west side of the country where you will find multiple tourist attractions. From the beautiful coasts to the incredible countryside you can't go wrong anywhere in Italy. The Coliseum is one of the most famous ruin sites in the world. Another honorable mention is the Pantheon located in Rome as well. Italy's national currency is the Euro. Currently one USD is equal to one Euro, which is makes financial planning much easier for trips! Trains are by far the best form of transportation as they are the cost effective and there are rails connecting all big cities in Italy. Finally the highest ranking hotel in Rome is the Hotel Eden which has been open since 1889!
