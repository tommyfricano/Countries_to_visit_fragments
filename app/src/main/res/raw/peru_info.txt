Peru is located in on the west coast in South America with a population of 33.5 million people. Their capital city is Lima which is located on the coast. The north part of the country just barely touches the equator making the climate fairly warm year round. There are very unique attractions within Peru that are most definitely worth the trip! Peru's top attraction is Machu Pichu which is an ancient town in the Andes mountains. Also, Machu Pichu is one of the seven world wonders. Peru's national currency are Peruvian Soles and the current exchange rate is 1 USD to 3.87869 PEN. The most popular and cost effective form of transportation are busses. The highest rated hotel in Peru is the Belmond Miraflores Park which has a cliffside view of the Pacific Ocean.
